George Brown College is a college primary located in Toronto, Canada, offering many programs in 
Arts, Information Technology, Business, Education, Engineering, Health Sciences, Hospitality, and more.
The college offers credentials such as Diplomas, Advanced Diplomas, and Bachelor's Degrees.
George Brown College offers in-person programs on the St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus, and the Waterfront Campus, as well as
offering a range of programs with online delivery via Zoom for domestic students.  
George Brown College offers a blend of theory and hands-on experience, prioritizing real-world 
education and applied research and field opportunities.
