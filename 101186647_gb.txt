George Brown College is a college primary located in Toronto, Canada, offering many programs in 
Arts, Information Technology, Business, Education, Engineering, Health Sciences, Hospitality, and more.
The college offers credentials such as Diplomas/Advanced Diplomas, Bachelor's Degrees, and postgraduate programs as well.
George Brown College offers in-person programs on the St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus, and the Waterfront Campus, as well as
offering a range of programs via online delivery for domestic students.  George Brown College offers a blend of theory and hands-on
experience, prioritizing real-world education and applied research and field opportunities.
